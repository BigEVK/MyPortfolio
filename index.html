About Me

I recently completed Rice University's Full Stack Coding Bootcamp and am looking forward to starting my career in Web Development. I have been interested in working with computers since I was a kid and my dad taught programming at a vocational school. There was a mainframe in a room the size of a small house. Oh how times have changed.

I have always had in interest in programming and have watched the internet grow over the years. When I was in college I was on several BBS's, played online games, and did a little web design. I ventured in another direction in the following years, but curiosity has pulled me back in, and my how web design has grown. I look forward to being a part of shaping its future, as I am sure it will shape mine.
